# Shootable doors

### How it works?
When someone shoots door (Game tries to place bullet hole on door) it will recive damage based on weapon stats.

## Door stats
### Heavy Containment Zone
- Health: 100
- Armor Efficiency: 99%

### Light Containment Zone and Entrance Zon
- Health: 100
- Armor Efficiency: 90%

### Prison doors
- Health: 100
- Armor Efficiency: 80%
